## 📖 Overview

**My AI Team** is a revolutionary creative collaboration platform that puts you in the director's chair with a full squad of specialized AI agents working for you. It's not just another AI tool - it's your personal creative studio where you direct, AI executes, and together you forge masterpieces.

---

## 🤖 Your AI Team

| Agent | Role | What It Does |
|-------|------|--------------|
| **💡 The Visionary** | Brainstormer | Generates 3 complete creative concepts with titles, descriptions, emotional impact, and techniques |
| **🔍 The Critic** | Evaluator | Analyzes all concepts, identifies the strongest one, and suggests concrete improvements |
| **✨ The Master Artist** | Refiner | Transforms the best concept into a breathtaking final masterpiece |

---

## ✨ Features

- **🎯 Complete Creative Pipeline** – Brainstorm → Critique → Refine in one seamless flow
- **🎨 Full Creative Control** – Choose shape, style, mood, colors, creativity level, and feedback
- **🔬 Multi-Model Comparison** – Compare responses from LLaMA 3.3, 3.2, and 3.1
- **📊 Export Options** – Download masterpieces in JSON, Markdown, or Plain Text
- **💾 Auto-Save** – Every session is automatically preserved in `masterpieces/` folder
- **🎭 Stunning UI** – Animated gradients, cinematic design, and immersive experience
